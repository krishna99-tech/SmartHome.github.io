Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app compatible with all smart home devices?
No, this app is specifically designed for devices developed by our company. It offers seamless integration with our ecosystem but may not work with third-party devices.
How do I update to a newer version?
Simply download the latest APK from this page and install it. The new version will replace your existing installation while preserving your settings and data.
Is my data secure with this app?
Yes, we take security seriously. All communication between the app and your devices is encrypted, and we don't collect personal data without your explicit consent.
What should I do if the app crashes or doesn't work properly?
First, try reinstalling the app. If the problem persists, try an older version from this page or contact our support team with details about your device and the issue.
